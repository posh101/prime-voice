Transforming Lives: Empowering Women and Girls for a Balanced Future

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision
To localize No. 5 of the United Nations sustainable Development Goals through the advocate for the total empowerment of women and girls who are widows, indigent, domestically and sexually violated, by training them on different skills and carrying out mental health check/therapy to foster well balanced mothers/future mothers, children, communities and societies at large.
The total transformation of women and the girl child through education, mental therapy and empowerment for economic/financial freedom.
To promote the No.5 UN SDG, through assisting women and girls discover who they are, strengthening them and making them whole(economically sufficient, mentally sound, emotionally complete)
